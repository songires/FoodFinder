About Us

Food Finder Inc. was established in 2015 with the aim of providing the ever increasing population of Bloomington with ease to access the internet for finding a place nearby to suffice thier appetite. Established by 3 students of Indiana Unversity, Bloomington, It has its Headquarters situated at Bloomington, Indiana. It is a website that has become very popular in 10 months of its start. It is headed by Kushal Sheth, Dean Ericson and Suraj Songire.
Food Finder Inc. was named among top 100 startups to work at by the FORTUNE magazine. In January 2015, it had 60 employees at its bloomington office which now has 178 employees and the number is growing. Now the website will also cover 16 major cities of the United States including New York, Chicago and San Francisco.
